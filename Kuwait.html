Kuwait

To the average traveler, Kuwait may ring a bell about ‘oil’. But embark upon a journey to explore this Western Asian land & you will fall in love with its architectural landscape, traditional Arabian hospitality, vast desert, peaceful beaches & lip-smacking food.

Top places to visit in Kuwait

  • Hawalli

    Also spelled as ‘Hawally’ & a former home to innumerable Palestinians, Hawalli is one of the six Kuwaiti provinces. It is this geographic location makes it so beautiful.

    View More

  • Salmiya

    A heaven for shopaholics, Salmiya provides unending opportunities to shop till you drop, with plenty of malls & arcades. Get everything you want from here.

    View More

  • Al Shaheed Park

    Yet another of the special places to visit in Kuwait is this urban park, consisting of plenty of green areas,lake, paths, restaurants, and two museums!

    View More

  • Kuwait Towers

    Another of those Kuwait city attractions to visit, Kuwait Towers are a group of three towers that stand at a staggering height and are one of the major attractions in Kuwait.

Things to do in Kuwait

  • Reflect at the Mirror House

    Welcome to the only house in the world covered with mirror mosaics.A tour of this famous home also includes tea, cookies, and quality time with Lidia

  • Explore the Kuwait City

    For some traditional shopping head to one of Kuwait’s oldest markets. The bargains here are nearly unbeatable, and it’s easy to spend several hours wandering here.

  • Shop at Avenue Mall

    You can quite literally shop ‘til you drop at Kuwait’s largest mall which features around 1,100 stores along with everything from cinemas to spas and hotels.

  • Go on a Desert Safari

    Be sure to take advantage of this bucket list activity during your time in Kuwait. These seasonal safaris can typically be enjoyed between October and March.
